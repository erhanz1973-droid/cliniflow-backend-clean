Connect WhatsApp with Meta

Connect with Meta (recommended)

  1. Click Connect with Meta on this page
  2. Log in and approve Clinifly in the Meta popup
  3. Select your WhatsApp Business account and phone number
  4. We save Phone Number ID, WABA ID, display number, and token automatically
If Meta login is unavailable on your server, use Can't use Meta? Request manual setup or Advanced Meta Setup only when you already have API credentials.

Requirements

  • A Meta Business account with WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API) access.
  • A phone number added in WhatsApp Manager.
  • Admin access to that business — same Facebook login you use in Meta Business Suite.

Advanced manual setup

  • Open WhatsApp Manager → API setup (or your number → API).
  • Copy the numeric Phone number ID (not your public +90 line).
  • Paste it here → Verify with Meta → Confirm and connect.

Troubleshooting

  • Verify failed — wrong ID, token not set on server, or number not in your Meta app.
  • No messages in Clinifly — check webhook is subscribed and WhatsApp routing is ON for the number.
  • Your number stays yours — Clinifly only gets API access; we do not take ownership of the line.
